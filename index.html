Arriglen Company

Our story

We are a young industrious and energetic company with a unique team that will stop at nothing but to achieve perfection and full customer satisfaction. We specialize in human resource services and some of the services we offer include:
  • 1. Human Resource
  • 2. Team building
  • 3. Psychometrics/aptitude test
One of our main aims is to be a company that stands out above the rest. We plan on achieving this by sparing no expense in providing high quality goods and services so as to build a reliable clientele.
Why Choose Arriglen

Vision

To become one of the leading firms in East Africa by providing high quality services and building the Arriglen family.

Our Mission

Arriglen is a market oriented, development centred organisation that focuses on provision of high quality products to our customers while maintaining a healthy competition with our peers and a comprehensive work friendly environment for our employees

What we offer.

SERVICES

Arriglen limited has three companies under it which are:
  • 1. Arrinet company limited
  • 2. Arridesign company limited
  • 3. Arritender

ARRINET

Arrinet is a branch of Arriglen company limited dealing with all matters tech from Computer repair and maintenance to Software development. We specialize in networking by providing LAN cabling and Wi-Fi for Apartments and Corporate users which we may link to live CCTV surveillance as per request. We also provide fiber connectivity for large bandwidth

ARRIDESIGN

This a branch of Arriglen Company limited made of young but experienced, designers, programmers, editors and artists who enjoy what they are doing and will stop at nothing to achieve excellence as they showcase their talents

ARRITENDER

Its a branch of Arriglen company limited whose purpose is to efficiently acquire and distribute goods and services using fair prices; to achieve the highest level of customer service; yield the best value at least risk; ensure technical viability and carry out sound business practices with business ethics
